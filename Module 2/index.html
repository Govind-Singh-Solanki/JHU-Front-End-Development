Cricket

The inaugural Cricket World Cup (known today as the ICC Men’s Cricket World Cup) was held in 1975, but two years before that, the women’s World Cup took place. The England women’s cricket team actually took home the trophy, smashing Australia with a winning score of 279/3.

Football

Football facts tell us the first football game was played in 1869 between Rutgers and Princeton in New Jersey. At the time, the rules did not resemble the modern game we are used to seeing. Eleven years later in 1880, Walter Camp codified the game with a series of rules.

Baseball

Apparently, a player has an automatically increased chance of hitting a home run upon hitting the said spot. It sits in the area between 5-7 inches from the barrel end of the bat. When the bat makes a satisfying crack sound and there is less vibration, then it can be said that a player hit the sweet spot.
